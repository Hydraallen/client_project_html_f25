Tips for Effective Networking

Networking is about building genuine relationships. Here are some tips to help you succeed:

  • Be proactive in reaching out to professionals in your field.
  • Prepare a clear and concise elevator pitch.
  • Show genuine interest in the person you're connecting with.
  • Follow up after meetings or events to maintain connections.
  • Leverage platforms like LinkedIn to expand your network.

Informational Interviews

Informational interviews are a great way to learn more about a specific role or industry. Use the TIARA method (Trends, Insights, Advice, Resources, Assignments) to structure your conversations effectively.
